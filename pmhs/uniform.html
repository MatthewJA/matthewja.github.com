School uniform at Port Macquarie High School is compulsory and consists of the following:

Junior Students

Girls

  • Royal blue and navy check skirt
    Navy blue shorts
    Navy trousers
  • Royal blue polo shirt
  • White socks or plain dark stockings
  • Black leather closed in shoes
  • Plain navy hoodless jumper/jacket

Boys

  • Plain grey shorts
    Grey trousers
  • Royal blue polo shirt
  • Grey or white socks
  • Black leather closed in shoes
  • Plain navy hoodless jumper/jacket

Sports Uniform

  • Navy shorts
  • School P.E. T-shirt
  • White socks and joggers

Senior Students

Girls

  • Grey skirt
    Navy blue shorts
    Navy trousers
  • Plain white, short-sleeved, collared shirt
  • White socks or plain dark stockings
  • Black leather closed in shoes
  • Plain navy hoodless jumper/jacket

Boys

  • Plain grey shorts
    Grey trousers
  • Plain white, short-sleeved, collared shirt
  • Grey or white socks
  • Black leather closed in shoes
  • Plain navy hoodless jumper/jacket
