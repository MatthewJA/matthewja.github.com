Crowdastro

Crowdastro is my honours project at the Australian National University, supervised by Dr. Cheng Soon Ong. We want to use crowdsourced astronomy data from Radio Galaxy Zoo to train computers to cross-identify active galactic nuclei with their host galaxies.

A major outcome of this project is the development of an open source Python package for machine learning on radio survey images. Crowdastro is MIT-licensed and is available on PyPI or GitHub. Documentation is available on Read the Docs.
