Coffeequate

Coffeequate is a computer algebra system (CAS) for JavaScript. It can manipulate algebraic expressions, and solve and simplify equations.

It's a work in progress, but already works for a multitude of equations, and works great on linear and quadratic equations. We eventually plan to add trigonometry, calculus, and a variety of other fancy features.

Read more, or check out the code, brought to you by myself and the wonderful Buck Shlegeris.
